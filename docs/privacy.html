Section 01

Overview

Searchly AI ("the app") is a desktop application developed by Proliant Data. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data — or more accurately, how it does not.

Searchly AI is designed to work entirely on your local device. The AI model that powers photo search (OpenAI CLIP) runs directly on your computer using ONNX Runtime. No photos, embeddings, or personal data are ever sent to Proliant Data or any third party.
Section 02

Information We Do Not Collect

Proliant Data does not collect, store, or process any of the following:

  • Your photos or image files
  • AI-generated embeddings or vectors from your photos
  • Your search queries
  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Usage analytics or telemetry data
  • Crash reports or error logs
  • Device information or fingerprints
Section 03

Google Drive Integration

Searchly AI offers optional integration with Google Drive. If you choose to connect your Google account:

  • Authentication is handled directly between your device and Google using the standard OAuth 2.0 protocol
  • Your Google access tokens are stored only in your device's memory and are never written to disk
  • Tokens are cleared immediately when you close the app or click "Disconnect"
  • Proliant Data never receives or stores your Google credentials or tokens
  • Photo data from Google Drive is downloaded directly to your device's memory for AI processing and is never forwarded to any third party

Your use of Google Drive is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Section 04

On-Device AI Processing

The AI model used by Searchly AI (OpenAI CLIP, distributed by Hugging Face) is downloaded once from a public CDN and cached by your browser/app. After that, all AI inference runs locally using WebAssembly (ONNX Runtime).

This means your photos are never uploaded to any AI service, API, or cloud processing platform. The model runs entirely within the app on your own hardware.

Section 05

Third-Party Services

Searchly AI interacts with the following third-party services only when you explicitly use the corresponding features:

  • Google Drive API — only when you click "Connect Google Drive"
  • Hugging Face CDN — one-time download of the CLIP model files on first launch

No advertising networks, tracking pixels, or analytics SDKs are included in Searchly AI.

Section 06

Children's Privacy

Searchly AI does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not collect any personal information from any users, regardless of age.

Section 07

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
