Overview

Searchly AI is a desktop application built by Proliant Data. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data Searchly AI accesses, how it is used, and what we never do with it.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your photos or image data.
  • We do not collect usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry.
  • We do not create user accounts or store personal information on our servers.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

On-Device AI Processing

All AI processing — including photo indexing and search — is performed entirely on your device using the OpenAI CLIP model via ONNX Runtime. Your photos and search queries never leave your computer. No internet connection is required for local folder search.

Google Drive Integration (Optional)

If you choose to connect Google Drive, Searchly AI uses Google OAuth 2.0 to access your Drive photos. The following applies:

  • Your Google OAuth access token is stored in memory only and is cleared when the app closes.
  • We only request read access to your Google Drive photos — we never modify or delete Drive files unless you explicitly use the in-app delete feature.
  • We do not store your Google credentials on disk or transmit them to any server.
  • Google's own Privacy Policy applies to your use of Google Drive.

Local Data Storage

Searchly AI stores photo embeddings (numerical vectors, not images) locally on your device to enable fast search. This data never leaves your machine and can be cleared by removing the app's cache folder.

Children's Privacy

Searchly AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the application after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
