Privacy Policy for Photo Searchly AI

Last Updated: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Photo Searchly AI ("we", "our", or "the app") is a photo search application that uses artificial intelligence to help you find images in your Google Drive. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Information We Access

When you connect your Google Drive, we access:

  • Images from your Google Drive: We read image files to create searchable embeddings
  • File metadata: File names and basic information for display purposes

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Images are processed locally in your browser using AI models
  • We create mathematical embeddings (vectors) to enable AI-powered search
  • All processing happens on your device - no images are uploaded to our servers
  • Your authentication token is stored temporarily in server memory only

4. Data Storage

  • Server Storage: OAuth tokens are stored in server memory only and cleared when server restarts
  • Browser Storage: Image embeddings are stored locally in your browser for faster searches
  • No Cloud Storage: We do NOT upload or store your images on any cloud servers
  • No Database: We do NOT store your personal information in any database

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your data with anyone.

  • Images stay in your Google Drive
  • Processing happens on your device
  • No third-party analytics or tracking
  • No advertisements

6. Google Drive API Usage

Photo Searchly AI's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • We only access files you explicitly choose to search
  • We use access to read and delete files only when you explicitly request duplicate removal
  • We do not transfer your data to any third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising or marketing

7. Data Security

  • All connections use HTTPS encryption (in production)
  • OAuth 2.0 secure authentication with Google
  • Tokens expire and require re-authentication
  • No persistent storage of sensitive data

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Disconnect: Click "Logout" to disconnect Google Drive access
  • Revoke Access: Go to Google Account Permissions to revoke access
  • Delete Data: Clear your browser cache to delete local embeddings

9. Data Retention

  • Authentication tokens: Deleted when you logout or server restarts
  • Browser cache: Persists until you clear browser data
  • We do not retain any data on our servers beyond active session

10. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
